MEMBER Commands!

Here’s a list of commands available to all members on the server:

  • /spawn – Teleport back to spawn
  • /home [name] – Go to your home
  • /sethome [name] – Set your home location
  • /tpa [player] – Request to teleport to a player
  • /msg [player] – Send a private message
  • /shop – Opens the server resource shop
  • /joyedforlife – Teleports you to the survival world
  • /sellgui – Open the selling GUI
  • /rtp – Randomly Teleports you around Joyedforlife
  • /pw create/warp/delete - Lets you Create/warp/delete Pwarps
  • /warp [name] - lets you warp to admin locations
