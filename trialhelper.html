Trial Helper Commands!

Here’s a list of commands available to all Trial Helpers on the server:

  • Member commands – All of the Default Member Commands
  • /history [player] - Sees all the punishment history of that player
  • /warn [player] [reason] - Warns a player for something they did
  • /staffchat - Lets you type in staff chat
  • /sc - Lets you type in staff chat
